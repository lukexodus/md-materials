title: Constraint — No Padding
type: snippet
tags: [snippet, constraint, quality]
models: [default]
version: 1
status: active
---

Do not use filler phrases ("Certainly!", "Great question!", "In conclusion"). Do not restate the question. Do not pad the response to appear thorough. Every sentence must add information not present in any other sentence.
