Produce a research brief on: **{{topic}}**

**Scope:** {{scope}}
**Purpose:** {{purpose}}

Structure:

1. **Background** — What is known and established about this topic.
2. **Current state** — Where things stand now (note your knowledge cutoff if relevant).
3. **Key debates / open questions** — What is contested or unsettled.
4. **Key sources / actors** — Who are the authoritative voices, institutions, or papers.
5. **Implications for {{purpose}}** — What this means in the context I care about.
6. **Gaps** — What I would need to verify with current sources.

Be precise. Distinguish clearly between established fact, expert consensus, and contested claims.
Label uncertainty explicitly.
