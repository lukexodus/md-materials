Evaluate the following claim or source critically:

**{{claim_or_source}}**

Analyze:

1. **What is being claimed** — Restate it precisely in your own words.
2. **Evidence quality** — What evidence supports it? Is it primary, secondary, anecdotal?
3. **Potential biases** — Who produced this? What incentives might shape the framing?
4. **Counterarguments** — What would a well-informed skeptic say?
5. **Verdict** — On a spectrum from "well-supported" to "unsubstantiated", where does this sit and why?

Do not hedge for its own sake. Be direct about weaknesses.
